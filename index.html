MUGAVARI FOUNDATION

Mugavari Foundation connects non-profits, donors and companies in all
over India. We help fellow non-profits access the funding, tools, training and
support they need to get there education they need.

About Us

OUR FOUNDER SAYS

Every human being in this world is endowed with inherent
humanitarian warmth, which can be brought out to benefit others.
All it would need is favourable ambience to stimulate them. The
resultant exuding warmth will go on to become each one's identity
or address. Mugavari (means 'ADDRESS' in Tamil) endeavours to
achieve this lofty goal by imparting higher education to one and all.

WHAT WE DO ?

them throughout their years of study, even after receiving their degree

return to us,supporting us with a regular monthly donation and mentoring
new students.
